Screen translator, ChatGPT assistant and Google Circle-style overlay in one app

AI Translate & Search (IT) is designed for users who constantly read, learn and search on their phones. If you regularly take screenshots to translate them, copy text into translators, or switch to ChatGPT just to ask “what does this mean?”, IT removes all that friction.

Once enabled, the floating lens sits on top of your screen. Whenever you want help, you simply drag or tap, circle the area you care about, and IT captures that region. From there you can translate, run OCR, or send the content straight into ChatGPT (or another AI service in WebView) for a deeper explanation.

Typical ways people use IT

  • Translate foreign-language news, social media, comments and app interfaces.
  • Study from PDFs or textbooks by sending sections to ChatGPT for simplified explanations.
  • Understand technical documentation or code snippets without leaving the screen.
  • Use IT as a Google Circle-style assistant for screenshots and images.

Privacy-first by design

IT does not collect analytics or build profiles. The overlay and storage permissions are used only while you actively interact with the lens. Your content stays on your device unless you explicitly send it to an AI service like ChatGPT in your browser.

You can also clear or auto-delete your on-device history at any time, so screenshots or session links are not stored longer than you need them.
